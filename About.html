Science Olympiad


Science Olympiad is a competition where students get the chance to learn about different fields of science. CSF

CSF is a club that is a part of a much bigger organization. One of the major thing sthat we do is volunteer services. We help clean the campus and do various community projects. In order to be a CSF member, they must contribute for a certain number of hours. There are many CSF clubs all around Califonia. All of them are related and help one another in finding more volunteer opportunities.

Cyber Security Club

Cyber Security Club helps students in IT skills. The competition requires students to secure a virtual image that is given during the competition. They must then find vulnerabilities in the image and try to make the image more secure through various methods. Teams earn points for fixing these things and the team with the most points advances to the next round. This helps with knowing how to be more safe on the internet.
